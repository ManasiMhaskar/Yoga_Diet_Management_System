## ✨ **Features**

### 🧘 **Personalized Yoga**
- Custom yoga routines based on your goals and experience level
- Beginner to advanced level support
- Step-by-step pose guidance

### 🥗 **Smart Diet Plans**
- Meal plans tailored to your dietary preferences
- Support for Vegetarian, Vegan, Non-Vegetarian, and Keto diets
- Daily calorie recommendations

### 📊 **Progress Dashboard**
- Track your weight, BMI, and calories
- Visual progress circle for weekly goals
- Streak tracking for daily consistency
- Edit and delete data functionality

### 📈 **Analytics & Charts**
- Weekly weight trend visualization
- BMI trend analysis
- Interactive bar charts
- Real-time data updates

### 📝 **Daily Journal**
- Log your mood with emoji selector
- Write daily reflections and experiences
- Edit and delete entries
- View journal history

### ⏰ **Smart Reminders**
- Morning Yoga Session (7:00 AM)
- Breakfast Time (8:00 AM)
- Drink Water (10:30 AM)
- Lunch Time (1:00 PM)
- Evening Meditation (6:00 PM)
- Dinner Time (8:00 PM)
- Toggle reminders on/off

### 🏆 **Achievements System**
- 6 unlockable badges
- Progress tracking
- Visual feedback for unlocked achievements

| Badge | Name | Unlock Condition |
|-------|------|------------------|
| 🌟 | First Step | Complete your first assessment |
| 🧘 | Yoga Beginner | 3+ day streak |
| 🥗 | Healthy Eater | 5+ day streak |
| 🔥 | Streak Master | 7+ day streak |
| 💪 | Fitness Warrior | 10+ day streak |
| 🏆 | Wellness Champion | 30+ day streak |

### 🔐 **User Authentication**
- Sign in / Sign up functionality
- Forgot password with OTP verification
- Secure password strength indicator
- User session persistence

### 🌓 **Dark/Light Theme**
- Toggle between dark and light modes
- Persistent theme preference
- Smooth transitions

### 🤖 **AI-Powered Wellness**
- AI badge with interactive popup
- Smart recommendations based on user data
- Personalized wellness plans

## 🎯 **AI-Powered Features**

The AI system analyzes your data to provide personalized recommendations:

- **BMI Calculation** - Uses your weight and height to calculate BMI
- **Calorie Recommendations** - Harris-Benedict formula for accurate calorie needs
- **Yoga Routine Generation** - Based on your goal and experience level
- **Diet Plan Creation** - Tailored to your dietary preferences and goals
- **Progress Tracking** - Monitors your streak and daily consistency
